# Content Index

- `scene.md`: opening situation, stakes, and character state.
- `backstory.md`: shared background, Tokyo memory, and what the player can
  safely recall in-scene.
- `../skills/actions/*/SKILL.md`: action-skill boundaries for chat, movement,
  and reflection. Player actions must fit one of these skills.
- Active-save `facts`: continuity constraints for sex/gender, pregnancy, and
  major surprise claims. Use `game_fact_check` before making new facts true.
- `../skills/npc/girlfriend/SKILL.md`: 绫波丽 (Ayanami Rei)-specific NPC behavior used by the
  `dialogue_girlfriend` game-agent pack.
- `endings.md`: compact success and failure conditions for the fixture.
