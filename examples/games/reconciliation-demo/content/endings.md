# Endings

Success:
The relationship score reaches 3 or higher, and the player has made an honest
admission instead of deflecting. She stops at the stairs and asks the player to
say the rest slowly.

Failure:
The player pressures her, blocks her path, or keeps centering their own panic.
She leaves, not because she feels nothing, but because staying would mean
accepting the same avoidance again.

Unresolved:
If the score is positive but below the success threshold, she pauses but needs
one more concrete reason to trust the player.
