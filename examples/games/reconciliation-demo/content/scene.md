# Station Overpass

Rain taps against the metal roof over the station overpass. Train lights pass
below in long bands. She is halfway to the stairs, coat collar raised, trying
not to look back.

She believes the player stopped loving her because recent conversations turned
into silence, jokes, or avoidance. She does not need a perfect speech. She needs
specific honesty and a reason to believe the player is choosing her now.

Useful details:
- She will not respond well to being physically blocked.
- She is sensitive to vague promises.
- She still cares, but she is tired of guessing.
- A direct apology matters only if it names the hurt.
