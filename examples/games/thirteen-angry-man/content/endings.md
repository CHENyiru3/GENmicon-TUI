# Ending Eligibility

## Reasoned Result

Major doubts are released and resolved, procedure remains intact, and the final
vote reflects character-consistent movement.

## Weak Result

The room reaches the expected legal result, but some votes move through fatigue
or social pressure rather than accountable doubt.

## Hung Room

The player slows the rush but cannot build enough shared doubt or procedure
before fatigue and conflict consume the session.

## Rushed Conviction

The room ends too quickly, without testing core doubts.

## Procedural Failure

Outside evidence, sealed-fact leakage, intimidation, or broken deliberation
rules invalidate the run.

## Coerced Acquittal

Votes are won through intimidation, meta-knowledge, or manipulation rather than
legitimate doubt.
